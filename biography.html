Social Worker Alangudi Dr. 515 Ganesan (74) Life Note

Emergency Service

Emergency service is free (from 1968 to present). Over 10,121 deaths & corpses in Tamil Nadu and various states for 54 consecutive years by a single man. 4500+ pregnant women, 2500+ accident victims.

Charity Work

His charity took the injured in various incidents to the hospital free of charge with his own ambassador car and brought them to the homes of the owners. He did his service tirelessly even during the last Corona period.

108 Ambulance Service

He is responsible for starting the "108 Ambulance" service of the Indian government and has done 54 years of "Free Ambassador 108 Ambulance" as an example. His mobile number is available in all police stations in the district.

Indian Red Cross

Indian Red Cross LIFE MEMBER CARD awarded to the President of India (First Citizen) #Dr515Ganesan. "INDIAN RED CROSS" has given authorization to travel on all national highways of India without any hindrance.

Ambassador Car Ambulance

He used his Ambassador car as an ambulance before the government started the ambulance service. He was the first in India to provide emergency services in his car.

Gaja Cyclone Service

In 2018, he went to Karur (near district) to do his service despite losing his home during Gaja cyclone and continued his service for 45 consecutive days for the common people.

Awards and Recognition

So far, he has received more than 85 different social worker awards. He has 4 cars for service, 5 girls, and lived in a tiled roof house for 40 years.

Fundraising

He has collected and handed over Rs.1 crore for natural disasters (Kerala Floods, Nagapattinam Tsunami 2004, Nilgiris Floods, Karnataka Floods, Odisha Floods, Chennai Floods, Cuddalore, and Gaja Cyclone).

Doctor Degree

He has been awarded the "Doctor degree" of "Social Work" by the University of Bharath virtual in Karnataka for his excellence in social service.
